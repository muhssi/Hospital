OVERVIEW

Aster MIMS Kottakkal is an NABH accredited multi-specialty hospital that delivers a comprehensive range of preventive, acute and outpatient services. The hospital, located in downtown Kottakkal in Kerala is ideal for people seeking treatment for various ailments, because of its excellent infrastructure and our commitment to maintain the highest standards of safety, cleanliness, integrity and honesty. Good health is an unquestionably imperative requirement of all people, and it is our first priority at Aster MIMS Kottakkal. With cutting edge technology and top-notch facilities for Trauma Care, Intensive Care and more, Aster MIMS Kottakkal continuously strives to provide holistic care to patients. All the care centres in the hospital are equipped with the latest technology that aids us in providing excellent comprehensive treatment to patients.
